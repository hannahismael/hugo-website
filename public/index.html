Hannah Ismael

Broadly, I am interested in investigating how law and policy can be built to enable knowledge sharing, access to AI supply chain resources, and transparent model development. I hope to explore where existing frameworks fall short in creating an internet that meaningfully balances considerations such as privacy, transparency, security, and innovation. In the past, this has led me to focus on IP law, privacy regulations, and antitrust law. Additionally, in light of AI becoming a central priority in many national strategies, I’m particularly interested in how AI policy positions reveal underlying geopolitical concerns.
